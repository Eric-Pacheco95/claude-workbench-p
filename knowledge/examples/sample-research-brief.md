# Research Brief: AI Agent Orchestration Patterns (2026)

> Generated by `/research` -- this is an example of what the research skill produces.

## Key Findings

1. **Multi-agent coordination is an organizational design problem**, not an intelligence problem. The hard part isn't making agents smarter -- it's making them coordinate.

2. **Heartbeat patterns** keep stateless agents on track: periodic check-ins with identity, plan, assignments, and state location.

3. **Behavioral correction through configuration** (adding rules when agents misbehave) outperforms retraining or reprompting.

4. **Budget-as-governance** -- tracking spend per agent/task turns cost from a surprise into a control lever.
